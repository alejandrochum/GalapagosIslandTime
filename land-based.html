MOSQUERA
ISLET

Great island to see frigate birds, blue footed boobies, land iguanas, marine iguanas, Galapagos sea lions and swallow-tailed gulls. Snorkeling around these parts is spectacular. May people see parrot fish, mexican hog fish, sea lions, garden eels and white tip reef sharks.

On the cliffs located in the south-east of the island, there is a sea lion colony filled with pups and adult lions, swallow-tailed gulls and cactus finches are among some of the creatures we will see. In the afternoon we will go to Punta Carrion, You will have the chance to go snorkeling in these turquoise water and find sea turtles.

The giant tortoise is one of the most iconic Galapagos animals. No visit to the Galapagos islands would be complete without ticking them off of your bucket list, and El Chato tortoise reserve is one of the best places for that. They wallow in mud pools, crunch on green shoots, and are free to wander around this huge reserve.
